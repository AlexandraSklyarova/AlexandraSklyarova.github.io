Generators of Cyclotomic Subfields via Gaussian Periods

Honors Thesis • 2025

An investigation of subfields of cyclotomic fields using Gaussian periods and period polynomials. The thesis combines Galois-theoretic constructions with computational experimentation in Sage to identify structural patterns and formulate conjectures for prime-power cyclotomic fields.

An Exploration of Quantum Cryptography

Course Paper • 2025

A survey of modern cryptographic systems in the context of emerging quantum computation. Discusses threats posed by quantum algorithms and outlines quantum-resistant and quantum-based protocols (e.g., BB84, E91) and related post-quantum ideas.
