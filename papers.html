Generators of Cyclotomic Subfields via Gaussian Periods

Honors Thesis • 2025

An investigation of subfields of cyclotomic fields using Gaussian periods and period polynomials. The thesis combines Galois-theoretic constructions with extensive computational experimentation in Sage to identify structural patterns and formulate conjectures for prime-power cyclotomic fields.

An Exploration of Quantum Cryptography

Course Paper • 2025

A survey of modern cryptographic systems in the context of emerging quantum computation. The paper discusses the threat quantum algorithms pose to classical cryptography and explores quantum-resistant and quantum-based protocols including BB84, E91, NTRU, and the quantum one-time pad.

Introduction to Elliptic Curves

Course Paper • 2025

A comprehensive introduction to elliptic curves emphasizing both theory and applications. Topics include Diophantine equations, projective geometry, the group law on elliptic curves, torsion points, the Nagell–Lutz and Mordell theorems, and applications to elliptic curve cryptography.

Elliptic Curves over Finite Fields

Presentation • 2025

A presentation exploring elliptic curves defined over finite fields, including point counts, the Hasse–Weil bound, reduction modulo primes, and applications to cryptography. Includes discussion of classical results and modern conjectures such as Sato–Tate.
