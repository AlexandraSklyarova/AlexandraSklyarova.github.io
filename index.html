What I’m working on

A recurring theme is producing explicit generators for intermediate fields of $\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_n)$ and understanding coefficient patterns in period polynomials. I like turning “weird patterns” into statements that can be tested and refined.

Current focus

Cyclotomic subfields & Gaussian periods

Example: for primes $p \\equiv 1 \\pmod d$, study period polynomials $P_{p,d}(x)$ and coefficient families $a_i(p)$ to detect structure.
